Cryogenic and vacuum equipment

Founded in 1992, Holy Cryogenics specializes in the design and manufacture of high-vacuum insulated piping systems, dynamic vacuum pump systems, and related equipment for transferring cryogenic fluids.

DG Skid

Automation and Piping for process industries

Gas separation, liquefaction and purification

Founded in 2006, Zhongtai Cryogenics applies cryogenic technology to gas separation, purification, and liquefaction, with patented processes and proven solutions across LNG, air separation, hydrogen, olefins, and rare gas applications.
